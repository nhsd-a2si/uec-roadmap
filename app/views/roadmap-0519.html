Urgent & Emergency Care: roadmap for digital health and care services

From the Digital Transformation Portfolio

About this roadmap

Digitally-enabled care is a key part of the NHS Long Term Plan which seeks to ease pressure on staff, make funding go as far as possible and accelerate the redesign of patient care. Within the next ten years it is expected that there will be widespread access to digital NHS services with the aim of helping patients to better manage their conditions and giving clinicians better access to records and care plans.

This roadmap sets out our plans to provide the infrastructure, systems, technology and processes that enable the development of digital urgent care services. We will improve telephone and online triage and provide improved technology to support clinicians, so that treatment is better targeted and patients are referred to the right place, first time

For more information visit https://www.england.nhs.uk/urgent-emergency-care/
